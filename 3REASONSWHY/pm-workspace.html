PM Workspace — Coming Soon
The Product Manager Workspace will receive forwarded BRDs from the BA pipeline and enable:
1
WSJF Scoring
Auto-calculate Weighted Shortest Job First scores for each feature based on business value, time criticality, risk reduction, and job size.
2
Feature Prioritization
Drag-and-drop prioritization board with MoSCoW categorization and stakeholder voting integration.
3
Roadmap Generation
Auto-generate quarterly roadmaps aligned with OKRs, with Gantt chart export and Jira sync.
4
Stakeholder Reports
One-click executive summaries with confidence metrics, impact forecasts, and resource allocation estimates.
